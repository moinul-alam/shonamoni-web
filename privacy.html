01

Information We Collect

1.1 Information You Create

When you use Shonamoni, you may create and store the following content, which is stored exclusively on your device:

  • Child profile information (name, date of birth, gender, profile photo)
  • Growth measurements (weight and height records)
  • Vaccination records and schedules
  • Developmental milestone records
  • App preferences and settings (language, theme, units, notification preferences)

This data never leaves your device and is not transmitted to us or any third party.

1.2 Information Collected Automatically

We do not directly collect any personal information. However, our third-party partners may automatically collect certain technical information. This includes:

  • Google AdMob — Device identifiers (Advertising ID), device type, operating system and version, IP address (used to determine approximate country-level location), ad interaction data (views, clicks), and app usage data relevant to ad serving.
  • Firebase Crashlytics — Device model, operating system version, a per-installation identifier (not linked to any personal identity), and technical crash logs. This data is collected solely to identify and fix bugs.

All such data is collected and processed by Google in accordance with Google's Privacy Policy.

1.3 Information We Do Not Collect

We explicitly do not collect:

  • Your name, email address, or any contact information
  • Your child's health data — all such data stays on your device only
  • Precise or approximate location data
  • Photos beyond those you choose to store locally as a profile photo
  • Contacts or call logs
  • Financial or payment information
02

How We Use Information

Since we do not collect personal information directly, our use of data is limited to:

  • Delivering app functionality — your locally stored child profiles, growth records, vaccination history, and milestone data are used solely to provide the features you requested
  • Serving advertisements — Google AdMob uses device and usage data to serve relevant ads within the App
  • App stability monitoring — Firebase Crashlytics uses technical crash data to help us identify and fix bugs, improving the stability of the App for all users
03

Advertising

Shonamoni uses Google AdMob to display advertisements. AdMob may serve personalized or non-personalized ads depending on your device settings and applicable regional laws.

3.1 Personalized Ads

If personalized ads are enabled, AdMob uses your Advertising ID and usage patterns to show ads relevant to your interests. You can opt out at any time:

  • Android: Settings → Google → Ads → "Delete advertising ID" or "Opt out of Ads Personalization"

3.2 Non-Personalized Ads

If you opt out of personalized ads, AdMob will serve contextual ads. Some data may still be collected by AdMob for fraud prevention and frequency capping.

3.3 Children & Ads

Shonamoni is a parenting tool used by adults and is not directed at children. We do not knowingly serve targeted advertisements to children.

For more information: Google Advertising Policies.

04

App Stability & Analytics

Shonamoni uses Google Firebase Crashlytics to monitor the stability and performance of the App. When a crash or error occurs, Crashlytics automatically collects the following technical information:

  • Device model and manufacturer
  • Android operating system version
  • A per-installation identifier (a randomly generated ID not linked to your name, email, or any personal identity)
  • Technical crash logs and stack traces

No personal user data — such as your name, email, or your child's health data — is transmitted or stored through this service. Crash data is processed by Google in accordance with Firebase's Privacy Policy.

05

Data Storage & Security

5.1 Local Storage

All user-generated data — including child profiles, growth records, vaccination history, and milestones — is stored locally on your device using Android's secure local database. This data is not backed up to any external server by us.

5.2 Data Retention

Your locally stored data is retained on your device until you:

  • Delete a specific record or child profile within the App
  • Use the "Clear All Data" option in App Settings
  • Uninstall the App

Crash data collected by Firebase Crashlytics is retained for 90 days, after which it is automatically deleted from Google's servers.

5.3 Security

We rely on Android's built-in security model to protect locally stored data. No method of storage is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

06

Your Rights & Data Deletion

6.1 Deleting Your Data

You can delete all locally stored data directly within the App:

  • Open Shonamoni
  • Go to Settings
  • Tap Clear All Data
  • Confirm the action

You can also delete individual child profiles or specific records (measurements, vaccines, milestones) from within each respective screen.

6.2 Uninstalling the App

Uninstalling Shonamoni from your device will remove all locally stored app data.

6.3 Advertising Data

To reset or delete your Advertising ID, go to Settings → Google → Ads and select "Delete advertising ID".
