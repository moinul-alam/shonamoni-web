What Shonamoni offers

Everything a parent needs,
in one place.

Growth Tracking

Log your baby's weight and height over time. Visualized against WHO Child Growth Standards so you can see exactly how your child is developing.

Vaccination Tracker

Follow Bangladesh's official EPI schedule alongside private vaccines. Mark doses as given and get reminders so no vaccination is ever missed.

Milestone Monitoring

Track developmental milestones by age band — rolling, sitting, crawling, first words, and more. Know what to expect and when.

Nutrition Guidance

Age-appropriate feeding guidance based on DGHS and IPHN clinical guidelines. Localized food names and Bangladesh-specific program information.

Growth Charts

Visual weight and height charts plotted against WHO percentile curves. Understand your baby's growth trajectory at a glance.

Smart Reminders

Timely reminders for upcoming vaccines, scheduled growth check-ins, and milestone age bands — so you're always one step ahead.

Multiple Child Profiles

Add profiles for all your children. Switch between them instantly. Each profile maintains its own complete history.

Bilingual

Full Bangla and English support. Switch languages instantly from settings. Designed for parents who think in Bangla.

Dark & Light Mode

Comfortable viewing day or night. The app adapts to your preference with a beautifully crafted dark theme.
